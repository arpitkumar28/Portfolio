Problem Statement

Urban traffic systems struggle with unpredictable congestion and limited emergency response coordination. Ambulances and emergency vehicles face delays due to signal timings and lack of automated priority routing.

Solution Overview

GreenFlow AI predicts congestion with ML models, synchronizes signals, and dynamically activates green corridors for emergency vehicles. The platform combines a Flutter mobile command center, Next.js dashboard, FastAPI backend, Supabase/Postgres storage, WebSockets for live updates, and OpenStreetMap visualizations.
